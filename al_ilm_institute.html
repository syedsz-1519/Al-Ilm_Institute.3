What We Teach

Sacred Rules of Tajweed

The foundational rules that make Quranic recitation beautiful, precise, and spiritually fulfilling.

☀️
Izhar
اظهار • صاف صاف پڑھنا
Clear Pronunciation
When Noon Sakin or Tanween is followed by one of 6 throat letters, noon is read clearly without Ghunnah.
🔗
Idgham
ادغام • ملا کر پڑھنا
Merging
Two types: With Ghunnah (4 letters: ي و م ن) and Without Ghunnah (2 letters: ل ر). The noon merges into the following letter.
🔄
Iqlab
اقلاب • بدل کر پڑھنا
Changing
Noon Sakin or Tanween before ب is changed to a Meem sound with Ghunnah of 2 harakaat.
🌫️
Ikhfa
اخفاء • چھپا کر پڑھنا
Hidden Pronunciation
Noon is pronounced between Izhar and Idgham with Ghunnah of 2 harakaat. Applies to 15 letters.
〰️
Madd
مد • لمبا کرنا
Elongation
Natural Madd (2 counts) and Necessary Madd (6 counts). Duration varies based on waqf (stopping) status.
📿
Ghunnah
غنہ • ناک سے آواز
Nasal Sound
Nasal sound applied to Noon mushaddad and Meem mushaddad. Duration: 2 harakaat. An essential beauty of Quran.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling — answered with clarity.

Are classes recorded if I miss one?
Yes, most classes are recorded so enrolled sisters can catch up at their own pace. Please contact us for specifics on each course's recording policy.
What age group is suitable?
We welcome sisters of all ages! Our Noorani Qaida course is specially designed for kids aged 7–15, while all other courses are open to teens and adult sisters.
How do I join the Google Meet class?
After enrollment, you will receive a WhatsApp message with the Google Meet link and class schedule. Simply click the link at class time — no downloads needed.
Is there homework or assignments?
Yes, short practice assignments are given after each session to reinforce learning. Submissions are done via WhatsApp and reviewed personally by Sister Saba.
Will I receive a certificate?
Yes! Upon successfully completing a course and assessments, sisters receive an official completion certificate from AI ILM Institute.
Is there a fee installment option?
We believe Ilm should be accessible to all. Please reach out via WhatsApp to discuss fee structures and any scholarship or installment options available.
